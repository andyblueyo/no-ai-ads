! ---- HOW TO USE ----
!
! OPTION 1 — Subscribe via URL (Firefox or Brave only)
!   Firefox + uBlock Origin or Brave's built-in blocker both support
!   subscribing to external filter lists. Chrome's uBlock Origin Lite
!   does NOT support this feature due to Manifest V3 restrictions.
!
!   In Firefox/Brave: open uBlock Origin (or Brave Shields) settings >
!   Filter lists > scroll to bottom > paste this raw URL and hit Apply:
!   https://raw.githubusercontent.com/andyblueyo/no-ai-ads/main/no-ai-ads.txt
!
! OPTION 2 — Paste manually (any browser)
!   Works in Chrome too. Open uBlock Origin > Custom filters >
!   paste the contents of this file > Import and append.
!   Note: you'll need to re-paste manually when the list updates.
